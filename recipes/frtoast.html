French Toast

Description

French toast is one of the main go to breakfast ideas for most people on the weekend.

This recipe will make you feel like you are eating authentic French food, even if its not even French.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 Cup of All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 Cup of Milk
  • 1 Pinch of Salt
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Vanilla Extract
  • 1 Tablespoon of White Sugar
  • 12 Thicccc Slices of Bread

Steps

  1. Mix Flour, Milk, Salt, Eggs, Vanilla Extract and White Sugar into the Bowl.
  2. Soak that Thicccccc Bread in the Mixture.
  3. Cook the Soaked Bread on a Preheated Griddle or Frying Pan.
  4. Serve with Syrup and Fruit of Choice.
