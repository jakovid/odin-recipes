Baked Potato Pizza

Description

The Baked Potato Pizza is a wonderful dish that is perfect for any sports themed gathering or depressed Sunday afternoon.

In just under two hours you can have this meal ready to feed your family, friends, or just induldge in it yourself you big fatty.

Ingredients

  • 3 Potatoes
  • 6 Slices of Bacon
  • 6.5 Ounces of Pizza Crust Mix
  • 1/2 Cup Water
  • 1/4 Cup Olive Oil
  • 1 Tablespoon of Melted Butter
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Dried Italian Seasoning
  • 1/2 Cup Sour Cream
  • 1/2 Cup Ranch
  • 3 Green Onions, Chopped
  • 1 and 1/2 Cups Shredded Mozzarella
  • 1/2 Cup Shredded Cheddar

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 450F.
  2. Scrub and Dry Potatoes, and Prick with a Fork.
  3. Bake Potatoes on a Baking Sheet for 50 minutes to 1 Hour.
  4. Fry Up Some Bacon, and Don't Be Afraid to Make Extra. You Know You Will Eat It.
  5. Preheat Oven to 400F.
  6. Mix Pizza Crust, Water, and Oit Together. Knead Until It Looks Like Pizza Dough.
  7. Shape Pizza on a Pizza Pan and Bake for 5-6 Minutes.
  8. Combine Butter, Garlic Powder, Italian Seasoning, and Potatoes in a Large Bowl. Combine Sour Cream and Ranch in a Small Bowl and Use as the Sauce.
  9. Place Sauce, Potato Mixture, Bacon, Chopped Green Onion and Cheese on the Pizza Crust.
  10. Bake for 15-20 Minutes.
  11. Consume Immediately to Burn Mouth.
