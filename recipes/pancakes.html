Pancakes

Description

Nothing feels quite like a warm stack of flap jacks on a cold winter morning.

This recipe will make sure any guest will want to come back for me.

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 Cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 3 and 1/2 Teaspoons Baking Powder
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1 Tablespoon White Sugar
  • 1 and 1/4 Cups Milk
  • 1 Egg
  • 3 Tablespoons of Melted Butter

Steps

  1. Mix All Dry Ingredients Together in a Large Bowl.
  2. Add All Wet Ingredients and Mix Until Smooth.
  3. Preheat and Lightly Oil a Griddle or Frying Pan. Pour Pancake Batter on and Flip When Browned.
  4. Serve in a Massive Comedic Stack and Douse with Artifically Flavored Sugar Syrup.
  5. Pass Out into a Food Coma.
